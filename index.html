About Me

Hi, I'm Marcus Ruhinga, a passionate Software Developer and Computer Science student at Midlands State University. My journey is rooted in technology, driven by a commitment to craft meaningful digital solutions.

My main focus lies in developing intuitive, high-performing software systems that merge clean design with robust backend functionality. From full-stack web applications to scripting automation tools, I enjoy building impactful solutions that people rely on.

Alongside development, I'm deeply curious about the world of Networking and Cybersecurity. I'm actively learning how systems communicate, how to defend them, and how to build infrastructures that are resilient by design.

Beyond coding, I love diving into new frameworks, watching engineering talks, and participating in hackathons that challenge my creativity and logic. Every project, no matter how small, is a new opportunity to learn and grow.
